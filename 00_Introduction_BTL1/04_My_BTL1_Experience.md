# My BTL1 Journey and Learning Experience

This section documents my personal journey while preparing for the **Blue Team Level 1 (BTL1)** certification. The goal is not to describe the exam itself, but to highlight the **skills, mindset, and learning process** developed during the preparation.

This experience is intended to reflect how I approach cybersecurity learning from a **Blue Team and SOC-oriented perspective**.

I chose BTL1 because it focuses on **practical defensive security skills**, closely aligned with real-world **Security Operations Center (SOC)** environments.

Rather than prioritizing theoretical exams, I wanted a certification that:
- Validates hands-on investigation skills
- Emphasizes evidence-based analysis
- Reflects realistic incident response workflows
- Prepares candidates for junior SOC and Blue Team roles

BTL1 aligns strongly with how modern defensive teams operate.
