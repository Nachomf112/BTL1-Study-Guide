# What is the BTL1 (Blue Team Level 1) Certification?

The **Blue Team Level 1 (BTL1)** certification is an entry-level, **hands-on cybersecurity certification** focused on **defensive security operations**. It is designed to validate the **practical skills required for junior Blue Team and SOC roles**, rather than theoretical knowledge or multiple-choice exams.

BTL1 is offered by **Security Blue Team**, an organization specialized in Blue Team training and certifications.

---

## üéØ Purpose of BTL1

The main objective of BTL1 is to demonstrate that a candidate can:

- Detect suspicious or malicious activity
- Investigate security alerts
- Analyze real-world artifacts (emails, logs, files, traffic)
- Respond to incidents using a structured approach

Unlike many certifications, BTL1 does **not** focus on memorization. Instead, it evaluates the ability to **perform real tasks commonly found in a Security Operations Center (SOC)**.

---

## üõ°Ô∏è Blue Team Focus

BTL1 is clearly positioned on the **Blue Team side** of cybersecurity:

- **Blue Team**: Detection, monitoring, analysis, and response
- **Red Team**: Attack simulation and exploitation (not the focus of BTL1)

The certification emphasizes **defensive thinking**, incident investigation, and operational workflows used in real environments.

---

## üîß Practical, Lab-Based Examination

One of the defining characteristics of BTL1 is its **100% practical exam**:

- The exam takes place in a **virtual lab environment**
- Candidates must complete tasks similar to real SOC activities
- No multiple-choice questions
- No theoretical-only assessments

Typical tasks include:
- Investigating phishing emails
- Analyzing SIEM alerts
- Reviewing logs and timelines
- Performing basic disk and memory forensics
- Inspecting network traffic
- Documenting findings clearly

---

## üß† Skills Validated by BTL1

BTL1 validates core entry-level Blue Team skills, including:

- Phishing analysis and email header inspection
- Basic Threat Intelligence usage
- Log analysis and alert triage
- SIEM investigation fundamentals
- Introductory digital forensics concepts
- Network traffic analysis
- Incident response methodology

These skills align closely with **Junior SOC Analyst** and **Blue Team Analyst** roles.
